Meet Nick

I am a Junior Developer with over 1 year of hands on web and app development experience. I am happy to share with you my projects and a bit about me.

I was born and raised in Northern New Jersey, where my tech journey began during college. Initially, I pursued a degree in Education at Montclair State University, but switched paths when I craved a more challenging and dynamic field. I enrolled in a Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Rutgers University, where I earned a certification in Full-Stack Web Development.

After completing the program, I began my career as a Junior Developer at Strategic Micro Systems, an IT company in Parsippany, New Jersey. There, I contributed to a project that focused on enhancing productivity and streamlining data management by integrating APIs, developing user-friendly interfaces, and supporting client operations.

In addition to my work experience, I am passionate about continuous learning. In my free time, I like to workout at the gym, play guitar, and read about history.

I am now looking for new opportunities to display my skills in problem solving, teamwork, and web development to continue to grow professionally.

SMS Dashboard

This powerful Software as a Service (SaaS) application was co-developed to streamline data management and boost productivity for Strategic Micro Systems (SMS), an IT company based in Parsippany, NJ. By integrating six different APIs into a unified platform, the app provides users with centralized access to essential client information, significantly improving workflows for tasks like billing, client management, and in-house ticketing. In addition to API integration, the application also offers powerful features such as CSV data exports and database querying capabilities, allowing users to perform CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations for client data.
